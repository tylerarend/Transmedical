For All Patients

Welcome to Transmedical!

This is Transmedical, a help site for all transgender patients to find the care that they need with their specific accessibilty needs. From cost and location to wheelchair accessibilty and sensory inputs, Transmedical is here to help you find the best care for your transition goals.

Get Started

Mission Statement

Finding medical professionals with experience in transgender care may be hard to find for some, and nearly impossible for those who need accomodations that many places have yet to provide. As both a transgender person and a neurodivergent person, I have personally experienced many of the barriers that you may be facing to find the right care for you. Transmedical's mission is to relieve the stresses of finding the right medical professionals at the right cost as well as the right accomodations you may need to ensure that you recieve the best care possible.

Availiblity

Currently, Transmedical is run by one Software Engineer, Tyler Arend. With only one person involved with the website, Transmedical will be availible to the United States of America, where I am currently based, before other countries are added. In the future, I will work with others around the world to allow for all transgender patients to get the care they need across the globe. These are the next three countries that will be covered by Transmedical in the future:

  • Canada

  • Mexico
